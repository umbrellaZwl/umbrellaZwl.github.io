state的父子级

$state.go(‘my.profile’)
$state.go(‘^’)到上一级,比如从my.profile到photo
$state.go(‘^.list’)到相邻state,比如从my.profile到my.list
$state.go(‘^.detail.comment’)到孙子级state，比如从my.detail到my.detial.comment
